TF House Rules

5 goals win a game

2 games win a match (3 games win a match if both teams agree before the first game)

Players are not allowed to switch positions during a game

Teams must switch sides after each game

No two games can be played using the same positions (i.e. between games at least one team's players must swap positions)

The losing team can decide if they want to switch positions before the next game starts

First drop in is decided by coin toss, subsequent drop ins are performed by the team who last conceded a goal

If the ball is out of play for any reason, it is dropped in by the team who did not touch the ball last
